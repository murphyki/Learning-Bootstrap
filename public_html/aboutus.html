Who are we?

MurphyK Labs Ltd. is an Irish mobile & web software development and consulting company located in Cork City, Ireland. We will soon be an international company as we are currently setting up a sales office in London and Paris.

Founded in 2013 by CEO Kieran Murphy (BSc) after his obsession increased for all things web, iPhone and app-related… and that’s where the fun started creating high-quality, reliable and popular apps. In the short time that the company has been in existence it has grown rapidly and today employs 5 highly experienced and skilled people.

Since the very beginning, the company has been successful in gaining many reputable contracts and we pride ourselves in being at the forefront of cutting edge web and mobile application technology.

With combined experience in software development, design and back-end integration expertise, we know what works, and what doesn’t.

What do we do?

We are the creators of a number of high-quality, reliable and popular apps for iOS, Android, Windows Phone, Blackberry and Desktop platforms.

We provide a wide range of services such as: Mobile Strategy Consulting, App Development & Publishing, App Marketing & Survey consulting, Mobile Web App development, Hybrid App development and Native App development.

Give us a call today and our engineers will sit down and work out with you the best solution to fit your needs. We believe in working closely with our clients throughout the entire development process, from start to finish, ensuring we deliver exactly what you need.
